Sadanga Tourist Spots

Betwagan Rice Terraces

The Betwagan Rice Terraces are among the area's most remarkable sights. These terraces are masterfully carved into the mountains and continue to be used for traditional rice farming.

Focong Rice Terraces

Focong Rice Terraces are more picturesque if you're on an elevated vantage point because you get to see the wavy ridges better.

Fowa-as Falls

Fowa-as Falls lies on the remote municipality of Sadanga. It has a majestic height of 75 meters. Its raging waters carved a very wide and deep pool with icy-cold and crystal-clear waters. Betwagan rice terraces is located at Brgy Betwagan, Sadanga, Mt Province

Nabenngan Bridge

A stunning footbridge located in Sadanga that’s gaining social media attention nowadays or what the Philippine National Police named it 'The Peace Bridge".

Turtle Shell

Some describe the village as a “helmet surrounded by rice field” while others say that it is a “turtle shell”, scenic view, the river flowing on the side the village is a perfect picnic place
