Paracelis Tourist Spots

Aggonongan Lake

Balinsuogan Camp

Feel the tranquility of the world...come and visit BALINSUOGAN CAMP located at Bananao, Paracelis, Mountain Province

Canabo Falls

A 15 minutes drive away from the Town center and take about 10 minutes walk passing through the rice field and stream. Mabarotbut Little Volcano is a small muddy volcano formed like a mini hill.

Mabarotbut Little Volcano

A fascinating natural feature known for its bubbling mud springs and geologically active terrain.

Nansawang Falls

This waterfall offers a refreshing escape, featuring serene surroundings perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Siffu River

The Siffu River is the main river system traversing Natonin and Southern Paracelis. It also serves as a mute witness to change and the passing of time.

Simmacbot Viewpoint

The Simmacbot viewpoint in paracelis is one of its kind in the province. Given the mountainous terrain of the municipality, it gives a generous view of Mountain Province and nearby Isabela Province.
