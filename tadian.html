Tadian Tourist Spots

Baangan Escapade

A big boulderstone on top of a mountain at Barangay am-am overlooking the mountain ranges of the eastern part of Ilocos Sur where the historic Bessang Pass and Tirad Pass are located.

Enchanted Eel

These mystical creatures continue to attract the interests of tourists and travelers. They are found on springs at barangays Cagubatan and Lenga.

Gao Falls

This is the literal chasing waterfalls, fom one breathtaking cascades to another, giving you a pure blissful adventure.

Gawaan Lake

Gawaan Lake is situated nearby to the village Banaao, as well as near Cagubatan.

Inudey Falls

Inodey Falls emerges as a site to behold, enchanting nature lovers and families alike with its breathtaking beauty and wonder. Nestled at the boundary between Tadian and Bauko in Mountain Province

Layog Farm

This was the highest permaculture farm in Tadian (around 900 meters above sea level) and going up and down the mountain slopes was very challenging!

Ub-ubanan Falls

"Ub-ubanan Falls" is located in Barangay Sumadel, a 30 minute drive away from Poblacion. The area is a favorite picnic ground for locals and nature lovers.

Mt. Mogao

Mt. Mog-ao stands majestically amidst the rolling plains, making it one of the popular tourist spots in Tadian, Mountain Province.
